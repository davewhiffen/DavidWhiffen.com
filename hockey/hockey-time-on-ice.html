    Time on Ice (2014)

    Tracker

    I added Pointstreak's Time on Ice app to our system.

    It meant we'd know when and how long every player was on the ice for, as well as a +/- of their shots and goals while on the ice.

    It allowed us to calculate player efficiency, and confirmed when line pairings were not working.
